COSE encrypt,

Struct for conversion to both the COSE encrypt and COSE encrypt0 objects.

https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc8152#section-5

This struct produces the following CDDL:

COSE_Encrypt = [
Headers,
ciphertext : bstr / nil,
recipients : [+COSE_recipient]
]
